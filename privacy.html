Privacy Policy for NAATI Vocab

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Introduction

NAATI Vocab ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

NAATI Vocab is an offline application that does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics
  • Browsing history
  • Any other personal data

Local Data Storage:

The app stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • App theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Accent color selection
  • Flashcard text size settings
  • Vocabulary sorting preferences

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Is controlled entirely by you
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

NAATI Vocab does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed. All app settings are stored locally using Apple's standard secure storage mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
